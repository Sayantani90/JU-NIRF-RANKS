Executive Summary

Jadavpur University has experienced a decline in the Engineering Category of NIRF Rankings, dropping from Rank #12 in 2024 to Rank #18 in 2025. Despite improvements in certain parameters like Teaching, Learning & Resources (TLR), significant challenges in Graduation Outcomes and Outreach & Inclusivity have affected the overall position.
